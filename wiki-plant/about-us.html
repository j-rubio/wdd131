About Wiki-Plant

Wiki-Plant aims to be an independent source of knowledge about natural plant extracts. The primary purpose of this site is to provide solid and reliable information about a handful of highly demanded plant extracts, including their origin, harvesting seasons, and methods.

In addition, it gives scientifically backed specifications and their applications to improve overall health and remedy some illnesses.

If you are interested in learning about plant extracts and trying to find the right plant-based product to improve health challenges, you are already part of the Wiki-Plant audience.
